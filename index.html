this was the beginning.

not of a straight line, but of many chapters.

many versions.

odel has been many people.

different cities. different rooms. different ways of seeing the world.

each phase teaching him something new.

✈︎      ✈︎      ✈︎

there was san francisco.

there was hawaii.

places that made him who he is today.

and then, tokyo!

not as a destination, but as a chapter. an exchange. a becoming.

he met so many incredible people.

people who felt rare. warm. unforgettable.

but what made them special wasn’t just the place.

it was him.

by me, and by the incredible people who get to be around you every day.

happy 21st birthday.

i’m so proud of you.

thank you for being exactly who you are.
